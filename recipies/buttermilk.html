Buttermilk Pancake Recipie

Home

Description

These buttermilk pancakes are considered to be the best and absolute favorite of the people over on the allrecipies website with many reviewers speaking about how delicious and soft and fluffy this pancake recipie is along with how easy it is to make them.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together in a large bowl
  2. Mix the wet ingredients together in a separate bowl
  3. Add the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients
  4. Mix ingredients with a spoon or fork until mix is well blended
  5. Scoop mix onto a hot and oiled pan until pancake is bubbly on top
  6. Flip with a spatula until both sides are brown
  7. Repeat previous steps for as many desired pancakes
